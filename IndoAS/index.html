About

 Indo Athletic Society ( IAS ) is nonprofit making government 
                registered biggest Social & Sports NGO in INDIA , More than
                 10000+ Cyclist, Runners, Trekkers   & Swimmers associated 
                 with us , Mission is work for health & environment , Purpose
                  is to serve nation  with green & healthy community.The main 
                  Objective of this is to be health and be safe. As well as exercising 
                  many muscles, athletics builds mental strength given 
                  the new situations the mind has to cope with. An approach that is
